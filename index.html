Applying to college can be the one of the most stressful events in a high school student's life. However, what can be even more stressful is transitioning into college life, especially if one decides to move away from one's home. Counslr aims to not only help make this transition as smooth as possible, but to also help students make the most of their college experience.

To achieve this goal, we aim to create a vast network of students at colleges across the United States to share their stories and advise prospective college students on different aspects of college life. This would include articles and videos by juniors and seniors at various colleges as well as online chat sessions and local meetups where college students and high schoolers can network.
